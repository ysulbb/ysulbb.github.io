Longfei Shangguan
Researcher, Microsoft Cloud&AI
Email: longfei.shangguan@microsoft.com
I'm a researcher at Microsoft Cloud&AI, Bellevue. Before joining Microsoft, I did three years postdoc at Princeton University, working with Prof. Kyle Jamieson on wireless and mobile systems. I received my Ph.D degree in 2015 at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof.Yunhao Liu and Prof. Ke Yi.
